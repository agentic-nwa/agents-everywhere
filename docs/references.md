# References

Books, people, and initiatives worth knowing about — passed around the
room and worth surfacing here.

## Books
- **[On Intelligence](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/243969.On_Intelligence)** — Jeff Hawkins. Theory of how the neocortex predicts its way to intelligence.
- **[A Thousand Brains](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/54503521-a-thousand-brains)** — Jeff Hawkins. Follow-up arguing the cortex runs thousands of parallel reference-frame models of the world.

## Initiatives
- **[Wisconsin Franchise Search](https://apps.dfi.wi.gov/apps/FranchiseSearch/MainSearch.aspx)** — Wisconsin Department of Financial Institutions' public database for looking up franchise registrations by name — a surprisingly useful source of franchise disclosure documents (FDDs) for market research.
